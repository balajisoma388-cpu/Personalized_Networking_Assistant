# Personalized_Networking_Assistant
The Personalized Networking Assistant is an AI-powered web application that helps users generate smart, tailored conversation starters for professional or social networking events. It uses DistilBERT  for extracting themes from event descriptions and GPT-2 for generating context-aware conversation prompts.
👥 Professional connections — helping you reach out to colleagues, mentors, or industry leaders.

🌐 Social networking — managing introductions, keeping track of friends, or following up after events.

🎯 Goal-driven networking — for example, finding collaborators for a project, expanding into a new industry, or building a personal brand.

A personalized assistant could help you with things like:

Drafting tailored outreach messages or follow-ups.

Remembering key details about people (interests, last conversation, mutual connections).

Suggesting introductions or opportunities based on your goals.

Keeping track of events, meetings, and reminders to reconnect.

To shape this into something actionable, I’d need to know:

Who you want to network with (professionals, friends, industry peers, etc.).

What your main goal is (career growth, collaboration, social expansion, etc.).

How you’d like the assistant to support you (emails, reminders, conversation prep, etc.).

For example, if your goal is career growth in AI, the assistant could:

Suggest relevant LinkedIn connections.

Draft personalized outreach emails.

Remind you to follow up after conferences.

Keep notes on conversations so you always sound prepared.
